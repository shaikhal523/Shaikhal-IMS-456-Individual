WHAT IS HOBBY TO MYSELF

For me,
A hobby is considered to be a regular activity that is done for enjoyment, typically during one's leisure time.
THobbies include collecting themed items and objects, engaging in sports pursuits, or pursuing other amusements.

Archery

Archery improves my mental concentration, upper body balance and strength, and hand-eye coordination. All I need to find is an archery range and I'm good to go – archery ranges typically have equipment for rent.

Bowling

Bowling is a great hobby, especially if play with my friends or family members to join on a regular basis. The indoor sport improves my balance and flexibility. And obviously, I also strengthen your muscles – specifically, muscles in the arms and wrists.

Camping

Camping is the best way to explore nature. It allows you to get away from the chaos in the city and gives you the peaceful environment you desperately need to relax and recharge. However, in order to fully enjoy camping, you need to have the right equipment. You can’t just go camping with a sleeping bag meant for home use. Take the time to shop for quality camping equipment such as tents, camping pillows and water filters, especially if you plan to go on extended camping trips.
